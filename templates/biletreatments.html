How bile duct cancer is treated

In cancer care, different types of doctors often work together to create a patient’s overall treatment plan that combines different types of treatments. This is called a multidisciplinary team. Cancer care teams include a variety of other health care professionals, such as physician assistants, nurse practitioners, oncology nurses, social workers, pharmacists, counselors, dietitians, and others. The common types of treatments used for bile duct cancer are described below. Your care plan also includes treatment for symptoms and side effects, an important part of cancer care.

Treatment options and recommendations depend on several factors, including:

  • The type and stage of cancer
  • Possible side effects
  • The person’s preferences and overall health

Types of Medication

  • Chemotherapy
  • Targeted therapy
  • Immunotherapy
