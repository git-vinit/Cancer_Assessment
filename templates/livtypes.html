Types of Liver cancer

There are different types:

hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC) or hepatoma


is the most common type of primary liver cancer and it starts in the main cell type in the liver, the hepatocytes

cholangiocarcinoma,


bile duct cancer, starts in the cells lining the bile ducts (which connect the liver to the bowel and gall bladder)

angiosarcoma


which starts in the blood vessels. This is a rare type of liver cancer that is more likely to occur in people over 70.
